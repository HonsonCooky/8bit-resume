Hailing from what my friends affectionately call a 'singing cult,' my formative years were steeped in music and the performing arts. As I entered my late teens, curiosity beckoned me toward the world of digital technologies.

Armed with a knack for calculus and a flair for photography, I discovered programming - an art form that blends creativity with methodical precision. It's a canvas with rules, yet near infinite possibilities. Now, I've traded sheet music for syntax; my compositions resonate through elegant algorithms, harmonizing data structures and orchestrating efficient solutions. Whether it's a symphony of assembly code or a concerto in network protocols, I thrive on transforming theoretical concepts into practical applications.

With 3 years of industry experience as a Software Engineer, my technical specializations are currently anchored in TypeScript and C#; complemented by a wide array of integration technologies. I aspire to engineer highly specialized, robust, and efficient software solutions that operate close to the hardware, delving into the intricate concepts of hardware itself. My passion lies in exploring and implementing theoretical concepts to make them practically viable. I am particularly keen on advancing my knowledge in network technologies and low-level protocol development.

Today, I stand as a bright, bubbly, resilient, and reliable individual. I'm prepared to bring my distinctive mix of creativity and expertise to exciting projects. Are you ready for me? Welcome to my melodic journey through bytes, nibbles and bits!
